Frequently Asked Questions
  • 1. Will boycotting Chinese products make any impact?
    We understand that China is not dependent only on India for exporting its goods. But certainly India gets a huge share of Chinese export. Our belief is that boycotting Chinese goods shall help in 2 ways. Firstly, it will encourage fellow Indians to use more native products which will in turn encourage more indigenous businesses in India. Secondly, it will send a strong message to China that India is united and Indians can go at any length when its about the nation.
